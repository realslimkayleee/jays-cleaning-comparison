Inquiries.

A repository of shared knowledge for those who seek the velvet ritual. Should your question remain unanswered, the silence of our craft is the only remaining response.

The Sourcing Philosophy

expand_more

Our beans are curated through direct-trade relationships that prioritize the longevity of the micro-lot. We do not negotiate on price; we negotiate on quality. Each batch is a singular expression of high-altitude terroir, roasted in small batches under the watchful eye of our head roaster in McKinney.

The Velvet Hour Ritual

expand_more

As the sun sets, our space transforms. The lighting is dimmed to a precise lux level to foster introspection. We ask that guests respect the hallowed nature of this transition. Laptops are discouraged after 6 PM to allow for the tactile luxury of conversation and the slow consumption of our signature brews.

Brewing Methodologies

expand_more

We utilize custom-modified espresso machines and precision pour-over techniques. Our water is triple-filtered and mineral-adjusted to a specific TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) count to ensure the delicate acidity of our light roasts is preserved, never masked.

Private Engagements

expand_more

For those wishing to host a gathering within our walls, we offer evening buyout options. These are limited and require a formal proposal. Our space is best suited for intimate salons, quiet celebrations, and focused artistic discussions.

The Beyond Coffee Selection

expand_more

While our heart beats for coffee, our soul wanders. Our botanical infusions and curated wines are selected with the same rigor as our beans. They represent the periphery of our craft—elements that complement the coffee experience without competing for the spotlight.

Still Curious?

Reach out to our McKinney studio. We respond with intent and deliberation.
